The Hidden Dangers of E-Waste

Environmental Impact

Toxic materials like lead and mercury pollute soil and water resources.

Health Impact

Exposure to e-waste toxins can cause severe health issues in humans and animals.

Social Impact

Workers in developing countries face hazardous conditions in informal recycling.

Economic Impact

Improper e-waste disposal leads to loss of valuable recyclable materials.

Resource Depletion

Failing to recycle e-waste increases demand for raw material extraction.
