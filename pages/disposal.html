How to Properly Dispose of E-Waste

Improper disposal of electronic waste can lead to environmental pollution and health hazards. Follow these guidelines to ensure safe disposal:

  • Check for local e-waste recycling programs.
  • Contact your local waste management facility for disposal options.
  • Participate in e-waste collection events in your community.
  • Consider donating working electronics to charities or schools.
  • Remove personal data from devices before disposal.
