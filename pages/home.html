What is E-Waste?

E-waste refers to discarded electronic appliances such as computers, TVs, and smartphones. It is a growing concern due to its environmental impact.

Why is E-Waste Awareness Important?

Raising awareness about e-waste helps to educate the public on the proper disposal and recycling of electronic devices, reducing harmful effects on the environment.
